CINAHL® Plus with Full Text

A nursing and allied health research database providing full text for more than 770 journals.

Psychology & Behavioral Sciences Collection™ Full Text

Offers full text for 560 journals in such fields as emotional and behavioural characteristics, psychiatry & psychology, anthropology, and observational & experimental methods. Also full text intellectual disability journals.

PsycINFO®

Contains more than 2.4 million citations in psychology and related disciplines, also including the psychological aspects of fields such as medicine, psychiatry, nursing, sociology, education, pharmacology, physiology, ID, plus more. Citations from over 400 journals are available in full text.

MEDLINE® with Full Text

MEDLINE® with Full Text is the world’s most comprehensive source of full text for medical journals, providing full text for more than 1,470 journals.
