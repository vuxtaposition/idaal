St. John of God Hospitaller Services

St. John of God Hospitaller Services is an international healthcare organisation with over 250 hospitals and centres in 48 countries worldwide.

In Ireland, St John of God Hospitaller Services provides services for children and adults with intellectual disabilities. It also provides a range of child, adolescent and adult mental health services, and care for older people.

To search for information on a particular topic, go to www.idaal.com and click All databases or choose a particular database.
To search for a specific journal click Journals above.
The Irish Province also manages programmes in Malawi, Africa, and New Jersey, USA. For information on the St John of God Hospitaller Services in Ireland, with links to the Order's services worldwide: www.sjog.ie
